Diversity vs Redundancy

[0 ]Redundancy is repeating the same controls at various intervals. (WW1 Trenches System)

[1] Diversity is using a variety of controls in a random pattern. (WW1 Setting up machineguns or other detections/weapons watching no man's land)

[2] Defense in depth uses administrative physical and technical controls

[3] Vendor diversity is a method of defense in depth with technical controls
