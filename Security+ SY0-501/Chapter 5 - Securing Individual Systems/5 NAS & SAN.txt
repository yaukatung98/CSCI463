NAS (Network Attached Storage):

    [0] Runs over a standard Network

    [1] Shows up as normal shares on Network

Storage Area Networks (SAN):

    [0] SAN provides block-level storage

    [1] Fiber Channel (FC)

    [2] Host Bus Adapater (HBA) 

ISCSI:

    [0] Poor man version of SAN

NAS is file level

SAN is block level

SANs will either use FIbre Channel or ISCSI
